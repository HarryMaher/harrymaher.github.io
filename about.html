about

I was born and raised in New Rochelle, NY and went to school at Grinnell College in Iowa where I studied Psychology with a Neuroscience Concentration. Currently, I work as a Data/GIS Analyst at a data, analytics, and mapping firm in Seattle where we specialize in helping tribal, governmental, and non-profit clients identify, collect, analyze, and act on data. Some of the tools I use at work include Python, R, JavaScript, ArcGIS, Excel, and Adobe Illustrator/InDesign.

In addition to being an avid runner and spending a lot of my free time outside, I volunteer with the Transit Riders Union, Sunrise Seattle, and I help run the Seattle Stupid Hackathon, a Meetup group of folks who invent completely useless and highly creative stuff. It's an extremely low-pressure environment great for helping cultivate new skills and having fun with quirky people. Join us!
