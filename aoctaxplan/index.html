This is wrong, 70% of my income is much more than $0?

Alexandria Ocasio Cortez proposed a 70% marginal tax rate at income over $10,000,000. So the 70% kicks in above your ten-millionth dollar. So even if you make $10,000,001 in a year you'd only be paying 70 cents extra total in taxes that year under this fairly conservative proposal.
