Kondofy It!

Have you ever wanted to get rid of parts of various websites that don't spark joy? If you read the news online or visit Facebook, then this bookmarklet may be for you.

Just drag the link below into your bookmarks bar, go to a website, and click your new "Kondofy It!" bookmark. Marie Kondo will pop up and help you get rid of the bits that you want to get rid of. There will be a pink box around the elements you can delete. Click to delete them. Press escape when the site is sufficiently Kondo'd. See the video for an example.
