Here is a set of interactive set of maps intended to allow users to explore how lifestyles influence (or don't influence) health outcomes at the Brazilian state level. For my final project I used data at the municipal level from the atlas of human development in Brazil.
Data were obtained from the Instituto Brasileiro de Geografia e Estatística (IBGE) at: http://www.ibge.gov.br/home
This website was written by Harry in Notepad in 2015 and is freely hosted by Google Drive.
