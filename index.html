Featured project

FART PANTS

Flatulence Automatic Recognition Tool with the Public Alert Notification Telemetry system — overalls with a VOC sensor and Christmas tree lights that lit up on flatulence detection. The project also includes HAM FART (Harry and Morimoto's Flatulence Acoustic Radio Transmission): text encoded as fart tones and transmitted over HAM radio. Built for the Georgetown Steam Plant Science Fair 2025.
