FART PANTS

Flatulence Automatic Recognition Tool with the Public Alert Notification Telemetry system — overalls decked out with a VOC sensor and Christmas tree lights that lit up when they detected flatulence on the wearer. Also includes HAM FART (Harry and Morimoto's Flatulence Acoustic Radio Transmission): text encoded as 20 unique fart tones and transmitted over HAM radio using Dual-Toot Multi-Frequency. Built for the Georgetown Steam Plant Science Fair 2025.
